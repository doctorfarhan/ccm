About CCM Notes

← Back to Index

CCM Notes is a collection of evidence-based critical care protocols and clinical references designed for intensivists, emergency medicine physicians, and critical care practitioners.

This resource provides quick-reference protocol cards and comprehensive clinical notes covering essential topics in intensive care medicine, from sepsis management and mechanical ventilation to vasopressor dosing and acute coronary syndromes.

Purpose & Disclaimer

These resources are provided for educational purposes only. All protocols and clinical references should be verified against your local formulary, institutional guidelines, and current evidence-based practice standards.

The materials presented here represent a synthesis of published guidelines and clinical evidence, including references from the Surviving Sepsis Campaign, American Heart Association ACLS guidelines, and major critical care trials. However, clinical practice evolves rapidly, and individual patient care must always be tailored to specific circumstances.

Important: These materials are not a substitute for professional medical judgment. Always consult current evidence, institutional protocols, and senior colleagues when managing critically ill patients.
