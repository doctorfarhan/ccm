Monroe-Kellie Doctrine
The cranium is a rigid, fixed-volume box. Volume = Brain (80%) + Blood (10%) + CSF (10%). Increase in any component must be compensated by reduction in another ‚Äî once compensatory mechanisms are exhausted, ICP rises exponentially.

CPP = MAP ‚àí ICP
Target CPP: 60‚Äì70 mmHg
CPP <50 mmHg ‚Üí cerebral ischemia
CPP >70 mmHg ‚Üí ARDS risk (vasopressors)
